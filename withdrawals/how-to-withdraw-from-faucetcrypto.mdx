In order to withdraw from **Faucet Crypto**, you need to have accumulated the required minimum amount of Coins (or more) to withdraw, and have set up/own a crypto wallet. In this guide, we will use Solana (SOL) to illustrate how to withdraw **crypto** from your account to an external platform or wallet.

Follow the steps below:

1. **Login** to your **Account**
2. Scroll down the left-side menu to the **PROFILE** section, and click on the **Withdraw** option/page under it
