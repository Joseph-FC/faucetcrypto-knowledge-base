---
title: "How to start earning on Faucet Crypto?"
---

In order to start earning on **Faucet Crypto,** please follow the steps below. If you do not have an account already, refer to Q1 of the [Getting Started](https://knowledge-base.faucetcrypto.com/category/11-getting-started) Guide.

1. **Login** to your **Account**
2. Click on any of the various options under the **EARN COINS** section at the left side menu to start earning

</Frame>

<Note>
  **Tip: **_For every task you complete, you'll earn both Coins and Experience Points (EXP) to level up to get more bonuses and a chance for an item drop\!_
</Note>
