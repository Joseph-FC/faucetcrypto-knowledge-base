title: "How to register on Faucet Crypto?"
sidebarTitle: "How to register"
---

To register on **Faucet Crypto**, you will need a valid email address and a very strong password.

1. **Go** to [faucetcrypto.com](http://faucetcrypto.com)
2. At the _"Get Started"_ box, continue with either your **Google account** or **X** (formerly Twitter) account; or select "**_Email & Password_**" if you want to proceed with creating an account manually.

<Frame>
  ![Faucetcrypto Signup Pn](/images/faucetcrypto-signup.png)
</Frame>

3. Fill in your _Name_, _Email_, \_Password, \_and re-enter your chosen password

<Frame>
  ![Faucetcrypto Signup Modal Pn](/images/faucetcrypto-signup-modal.png)
</Frame>

4. Read the [Terms of Service](https://faucetcrypto.com/terms-of-service) & [Privacy Policy](https://faucetcrypto.com/privacy-policy), and agree to it by ticking the specified box.
5. Then click on the **_Register_** button to complete the process.

<Note>
  **Tips:** _When signing up, never use a password you have already used to signup on other sites. Always practice the use of randomly generated passwords_
</Note>
