title: "How to reset or change password?"
---

You can reset your Account's password in two (2) ways:

- On the **login page**, by clicking on "**_Forgot Password_**" and following the instructions.
- Changing it via your Profile settings.

---

### How to change your password via profile settings

To change your password via profile settings, please follow the instructions below:

1. **Login** to your **Account**
2. Click on the **Settings** icon at the bottom of the side menu and select "**_My Account_**" to access your Profile page

3. At your **Profile page**, scroll down till you find the "**_Update Password_**" box. Fill in the respective areas and click "**_Save_**"
