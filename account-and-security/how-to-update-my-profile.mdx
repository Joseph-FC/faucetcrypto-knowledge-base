---
title: "How to update my profile?"
---

There may be a couple of reasons why you may want to update your profile after signing up. Changing your username, password, favorite cryptocurrency, and securing your account can all be done through your Account Profile settings.

To update your profile:

1. **Login** to your **Account**
2. Click on the **Settings** icon at the bottom of the side menu and select "**_My Account_**" to access your Profile page

3. Once it opens, you will find the "**_Profile Information_**" box. Update the information you wish to, then click "**_Save_**"
