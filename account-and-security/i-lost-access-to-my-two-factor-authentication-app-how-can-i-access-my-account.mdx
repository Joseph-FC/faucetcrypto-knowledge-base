---
title: "I lost access to my Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) app. How can I access my Account?"
---

If you happen to lose access to your 2FA app or device, to recover or have access to your account, you will have to access your account using your **Recovery Codes**. These codes were provided to you when you first enabled 2FA security for your account.

Please follow the instructions below:

1. When attempting to log into your account, click on "**_use a recovery code_**"

<Frame>
  ![Faucetcrypto Recovery Pn](/images/faucetcrypto-recovery.png)
</Frame>

2. On the next screen, enter any of your emergency **recovery codes** which were provided to you when you first enabled 2FA security for your account. Then click on "**_Verify Code_**" to successfully login

<Frame>
  ![Faucetcrypto Recovery Verifycode Pn](/images/faucetcrypto-recovery-verifycode.png)
</Frame>

Not sure what a recovery code is? Please refer to [this guide](https://knowledge-base.faucetcrypto.com/article/41-how-to-enable-disable-2fa-security).
