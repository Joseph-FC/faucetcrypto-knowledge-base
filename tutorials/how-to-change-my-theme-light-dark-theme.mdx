---
title: "How to change my theme (light & dark theme)"
---

Both light and dark themes are supported on the website. You can quickly switch between them whenever you like by clicking the **Settings** icon next to your username in the bottom left corner of the side menu and choosing Light (or Dark) Theme as shown below:
