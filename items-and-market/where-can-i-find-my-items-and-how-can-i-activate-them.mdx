---
title: "Where can I find my Items and how can I activate them?"
---

All your collected Items are stored in your Inventory and can be accessed or activated at any time. To find your collected Items, follow the steps below:

1. **Login** to your **Account**
2. Click on "**_Inventory_**" at the top right corner of the top navigation bar
